The Greenspan Floortime Approach™ for Parents has three parts: an assessment by parents, video-lecture course by Dr. Greenspan, and four workshops.

The Assessment

The developmental and sensory assessments will give you a profile of your child’s strengths and weaknesses. With this you will understand the areas your child needs to strengthen and improve his/her development. You will be able to track changes over time.

Available 24/7, 12-months a year. If you take the Parent Course, you receive the Assessment free for one year for one child.
