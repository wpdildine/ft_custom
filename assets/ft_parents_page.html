Learn Floortime from Dr. Stanley Greenspan at Home and at your own Pace

The Greenspan Floortime Approach for Parents introduces the Floortime e-Manual and offers three complementary components: a Lecture Series explaining the developmental model behind Floortime, an audio Database with Dr. Greenspan’s advice on autism and child development, and a free Assessment. We offer these components as a package or individually and recommend that the e-Manual be completed first. There are also four workshops: Meltdowns, [The Learning Tree-name change?], Regulatory/Sensory Processing, and Floortime for Older Children and Adults with Autism.



The Floortime e-Manual

In the last ten years, experts have strongly recommended that parents participate in the treatment of their child with autism. The interactive, online e-Manual allows you to develop a critical eye for what your child needs. It hones in on the essential information so that you can understand, learn, and implement Floortime quickly.

The interactive, online manual uses short video excerpts from Dr. Greenspan to break down the steps of Floortime, and it demonstrates them in three types of play. It gives detailed techniques and practical information for implementing Floortime. It has an extensive section to help with problems you might encounter while doing Floortime plus many tips and insights.

Available 24/7, 12-months a year. If you take the Parent Course, you receive the Assessment free for one year for one child.
