For Professionals

The Greenspan Floortime Approach for Professionals offers an intensive video-lecture course and four workshops on Learning Differences, Sensory Processing Problems, Meltdowns, and Life-Stage Challenges. The lectures and video examples cover the Greenspan developmental model, the assessment approach, diagnostic factors, and Floortime intervention. In them you learn to:


  • Apply the Developmental Model to individual differences and relationship patterns;
  • Organize the components of an assessment;
  • Evaluate emotional and developmental delays and disorders.
  • Help children through using Floortime.
