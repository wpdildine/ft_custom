Professional Training and Certification

The three components of The Greenspan Floortime Approach Training and Certification Program offer a learning system for working with children with autism. These components progressively explain how to achieve his goals for children with ASD, which are to:


  • Deepen Relationships through meaningful interactions with the people in their lives.
  • Strengthen Communication by expressing wants, ideas, and opinions, not just repeating words.
  • Expand Thinking and learning by understanding concepts, not just facts.
  • Foster Better Behavior through understanding and self-control.

The first two training components—The Professional Course and the Floortime Manual—provide the basics of Dr. Greenspan’s developmental model and Floortime intervention. You can take them individually as well as part of the certification program (See CEU options for The Professional Course and The Workshops).


The Professional Course

The Professional Training and Certification begins with the lecture series that Dr. Greenspan chose for explaining his developmental model and Floortime intervention. It is an 11-hour online course available 24 hours a day to watch as many times as you wish. If you pass the mastery test, you receive a certificate of mastery for the course.

The Floortime Manual™

The Floortime Manual explores Floortime sessions, component by component, breaking down the steps and techniques of Floortime. It details practical information for effectively using Floortime in three types of play and offers interactive practice. It has an extensive section to help with problems you might encounter while doing Floortime plus many tips and insights.

Hands-on Training

The hands-on training gives professionals the practical guidance and assurance to work successfully with children on the autism spectrum. Certification training in The Greenspan Floortime Approach® is provided by The Floortime Center® directors, Jake Greenspan and Tim Bleecker. The multi-level certification gives practitioners the education and training they need in order to provide the highest quality Greenspan Floortime services. You will be trained in the same manner that Dr. Greenspan trained Jake Greenspan and Tim Bleecker.
